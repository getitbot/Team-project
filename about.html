About SkillForge

SkillForge is an initiative by IIT Delhi Alumni designed to make top-tier technical education accessible to everyone. We believe that financial constraints should never stop a student from learning.


Our Mission

To provide high-quality mentorship, internship opportunities, and industry-standard projects to students for free (via our refund model).
